What kind of service is this?

Madison Oil Medic is a mobile maintenance and inspection service focused on oil service, routine checks, tire rotation when safe, battery testing, fluids, filters, wipers, visible brake checks, OBD2 scans, and clear checklist-style notes. It is not a full repair shop. If your vehicle needs advanced diagnostics, major repair, lift work, or certified repair-shop service, I will say so clearly.

Can you repair a flat tire or slow leak?

Often, yes, if the puncture is in a repairable tread area and the tire is otherwise safe. I do not repair sidewall damage, shoulder damage, blowouts, severe tire wear, large punctures, or unsafe tires. If it cannot be safely repaired, I can help with air-up, spare tire installation when possible, or the next best step.

What makes the $119 Full Maintenance Visit worth it?
